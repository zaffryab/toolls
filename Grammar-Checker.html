How to Use the Grammar Checker Tool

  1. Enter your text in the input box above. You can type directly or paste text from any document.
  2. Click the "Check Grammar" button to analyze your text for errors.
  3. Review the highlighted errors in the output box. Different colors indicate different types of issues:
    • Yellow - Spelling errors
    • Pink - Grammar errors
    • Blue - Punctuation issues
  4. Click on any highlighted error to see suggested corrections.
  5. Use the "Accept All Changes" button to automatically apply all corrections.
  6. Copy the corrected text using the "Copy Corrected Text" button.

Tips for Effective Grammar Checking

  • Check one paragraph at a time for better focus.
  • Review suggestions carefully - not all automated corrections may be appropriate for your context.
  • Use this tool as a first pass, then proofread manually for best results.
  • For academic or professional writing, consider additional proofreading.
