How to Use the Timestamp Converter

1

Convert Timestamp to Date

Enter a Unix timestamp (in seconds) in the first input field and click "Convert to Date". The tool will display the corresponding date and time in your local timezone.

2

Convert Date to Timestamp

Select a date and time using the datetime picker. Choose your timezone (Local or UTC) and click "Convert to Timestamp". The tool will display the Unix timestamp in seconds and milliseconds.

3

Use Current Values

Click "Current Timestamp" to insert the current Unix timestamp. Click "Current Date" to set the datetime picker to the current date and time.

4

Understanding Results

Unix timestamps represent the number of seconds that have elapsed since January 1, 1970 (UTC). Milliseconds are also provided for more precise time measurements.
