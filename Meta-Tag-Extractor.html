Why Meta Tags Matter

Meta tags provide information to search engines about your webpage. They are crucial for SEO because:

  • Title tags appear in search results and browser tabs
  • Meta descriptions influence click-through rates
  • Proper viewport settings ensure mobile compatibility
  • Robots meta tags control search engine indexing
  • Open Graph tags enhance social media sharing

Well-optimized meta tags can significantly improve your website's visibility and traffic.

SEO Best Practices

Title Tags

Keep under 60 characters. Include primary keywords at the beginning.

Meta Descriptions

Write compelling summaries between 150-160 characters. Include a call-to-action.

Viewport Tag

Essential for responsive design. Always include: <meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1">

Open Graph Tags

Add og:title, og:description, and og:image to control how your content appears on social media.
