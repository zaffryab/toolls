❓ How to Use the Image Editor

1

Upload Your Image

Click the "Upload Image" button and select an image from your device. We support JPG, PNG, and GIF formats.

2

Adjust Image Settings

Use the sliders to adjust brightness, contrast, saturation, and blur to enhance your image.

3

Apply Filters

Choose from various filters to apply different visual effects to your image with a single click.

4

Rotate or Flip

Use the rotate and flip buttons to adjust the orientation of your image as needed.

5

Download Your Image

Once you're satisfied with the edits, click the download button to save your edited image.

6

Start Over

Use the reset button to clear all edits and start over with your original image.
