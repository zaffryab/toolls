How to Use the Keyword Density Checker

1

Paste Your Content

Copy the text you want to analyze and paste it into the input field. The tool works best with 200+ words for meaningful analysis.

2

Click Analyze

Press the "Analyze Keywords" button to process your content. Our algorithm will scan your text and calculate keyword frequencies.

3

Review Results

Examine the keyword density table to see which words appear most frequently. The density percentage shows how often each keyword appears relative to the total word count.

4

Optimize Your Content

Use the results to adjust your keyword usage. Aim for a natural density between 1-3% for your primary keywords. Avoid keyword stuffing for better SEO results.

Pro Tip: The ideal keyword density varies by industry and competition. Use this tool as a guide, but always prioritize natural, reader-friendly content. Search engines favor content that provides value to readers over content that's artificially optimized.
