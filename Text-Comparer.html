How to Use the Text Comparer Tool

1. Enter Texts

Paste your original text in the first box and the modified text in the second box.

2. Set Options

Choose whether to ignore case differences or whitespace variations between the texts.

3. Compare

Click the "Compare Texts" button to analyze the differences between your texts.

4. Review Results
