Sort, organize, and clean your text with our powerful online tool

How to Use the Text Sorter Tool

Step 1: Enter Text

Paste or type your text into the input area. You can enter any text with multiple lines.

Step 2: Choose Sorting Method

Select how you want to sort your text: alphabetically (A-Z or Z-A), by line length, or shuffle randomly.

Step 3: View Results

Your sorted text will appear in the output area. You can then copy or download the results.

Step 4: Clear or Start Over

Use the "Clear All" button to reset both text areas and start with new text.
