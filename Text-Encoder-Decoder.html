How to Use This Tool

1 Enter Your Text

Type or paste the text you want to encode or decode into the input field.

2 Choose Action

Click "Encode Text" to convert your text to encoded format or "Decode Text" to convert encoded text back to readable format.

3 Get Results

Your converted text will appear in the result field. You can copy it to clipboard with the copy button.
