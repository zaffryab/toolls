How to Use the Countdown Timer

1

Set Your Timer

Choose a target date and time using the date and time pickers, or use one of the preset timers for quick setup.

2

Start the Countdown

Click the Start button to begin the countdown. Use Pause to temporarily stop and Reset to clear the timer.

3

Track Your Event

Watch the timer count down to your event. The display will show days, hours, minutes and seconds remaining.
