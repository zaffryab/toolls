How to Use the Broken Link Checker

1

Enter Your Website URL

Type or paste the full URL of the website you want to check (e.g., https://yourwebsite.com).

2

Select Scan Options

Choose which types of links to check (images, anchor links, scripts). All are selected by default.

3

Start the Scan

Click "Scan for Broken Links" to begin the analysis. The tool will crawl your website and check all links.

4

Review Results

Examine the results to identify broken links. Fix or remove any broken links to improve your site's SEO and user experience.

Pro Tip: Regularly check your website for broken links (monthly recommended). Broken links negatively impact SEO rankings and frustrate users. Use this tool to maintain a healthy, link-error-free website.
