How to Use the Text Summarizer

Our text summarizer tool is designed to help you quickly condense long texts into shorter versions while preserving the key information. Follow these simple steps:

1

Enter Your Text

Copy and paste the text you want to summarize into the input box. Our tool can handle articles, essays, reports, and more.

2

Adjust Summary Length

Use the slider to select how concise you want your summary to be. Lower percentages create shorter summaries.

3

Generate Summary

Click the "Summarize Text" button. Our AI algorithm will process your text and extract the most important points.

4

Review and Copy

Review the generated summary, check the statistics, and copy it to your clipboard with one click.

Tips for Best Results:

  • Ensure your text is well-structured for better summarization
  • For longer documents, summarize in sections
  • Adjust the length slider to find the perfect balance
  • Use clear and concise language in your original text
