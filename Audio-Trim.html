How to Use the Audio Trimmer

Our online audio trimmer allows you to cut and edit audio files directly in your browser without any software installation.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Upload your audio file by clicking the button or dragging and dropping
  2. Set start and end points using the time inputs or by clicking on the waveform
  3. Preview your selection using the play/pause buttons
  4. Trim the audio when you're satisfied with your selection
  5. Download the trimmed version in the original format

Supported Audio Formats

The tool supports most common audio formats including:

  • MP3 (most common compressed format)
  • WAV (uncompressed high quality)
  • OGG (open source format)
  • AAC (used by Apple devices)

Technical Information

The audio processing happens entirely in your browser using the Web Audio API. No files are uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy for your audio files.
