How SIP Calculator Works

A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) is a method of investing a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds. This calculator helps you estimate the potential returns on your SIP investments.

SIP Calculation Formula

The future value of SIP investments is calculated using the formula:

FV = P × [ ( (1 + r)^n - 1 ) / r ] × (1 + r)

Where:

  • FV = Future Value of investment
  • P = Monthly investment amount
  • r = Monthly expected return rate (annual rate / 12)
  • n = Total number of months (tenure × 12)

Example Calculation

For a monthly investment of ₹5,000 at 12% annual return for 10 years (120 months):

Monthly return rate = 12%/12 = 1% = 0.01

FV = 5000 × [ ( (1 + 0.01)^120 - 1 ) / 0.01 ] × (1 + 0.01) ≈ ₹11,61,695

Understanding Your Results

Total Investment: This is the total amount you invested over the tenure (Monthly SIP × Months).

Wealth Gained: This is the profit earned from your investments.

Future Value: This is the total corpus you will have at the end of the tenure.
