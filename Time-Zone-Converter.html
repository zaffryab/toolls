About Time Zones

Time zones are regions of the Earth that have the same standard time. The world is divided into 24 time zones, each generally 15 degrees of longitude apart. The time zone converter helps you quickly determine what time it will be in another part of the world.

Common Time Zone Abbreviations

  • UTC: Coordinated Universal Time - The primary time standard
  • EST: Eastern Standard Time (UTC-5)
  • PST: Pacific Standard Time (UTC-8)
  • GMT: Greenwich Mean Time (UTC+0)
  • CET: Central European Time (UTC+1)
  • IST: India Standard Time (UTC+5:30)
  • AEST: Australian Eastern Standard Time (UTC+10)
  • JST: Japan Standard Time (UTC+9)
