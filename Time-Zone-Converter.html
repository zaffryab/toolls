How to Use the Time Zone Converter

1

Select Date & Time

Choose the date and time you want to convert in the "Convert From" section. The current time is displayed automatically.

2

Choose Time Zones

Select your current time zone and the target time zone you want to convert to from the dropdown menus.

3

Convert

Click the "Convert Time" button to see the equivalent time in the target time zone. The converted time will appear instantly.

4

World Clocks

Check the current time in major cities around the world with the live world clocks at the bottom of the tool.
