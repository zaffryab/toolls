Standard Tipping Guide

Restaurants

15-20% of pre-tax bill for good service. 18% is standard for good service.

Food Delivery

10-15% of the bill, or $2-5 for pizza delivery depending on order size.

Taxi/Rideshare

15-20% of the fare. Round up to the nearest dollar for shorter trips.

Hotel Staff

$1-5 per bag for bellhops, $2-5 per night for housekeeping.
