How to Use the GPA Calculator

  1. Enter your course information: name (optional), credit hours, and grade.
  2. Click "Add Another Course" if you have more courses to include.
  3. Click "Calculate GPA" to see your GPA result.
  4. Use "Reset" to clear all fields and start over.

Note: The GPA is calculated by multiplying the grade points by the credit hours for each course, summing these values, and then dividing by the total credit hours.
