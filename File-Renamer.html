How to Use the File Renamer Tool

1

Upload Your Files

Click the "Select Files" button or drag and drop your files into the upload area. You can select multiple files at once.

2

Configure Renaming Options

Choose how you want to rename your files - add prefixes/suffixes, replace text, or add numbering sequences.

3

Preview Changes

Click "Preview Changes" to see how your files will be renamed before applying the changes.

4

Rename and Download

Click "Rename Files" to apply your changes and then "Download All" to get your renamed files in a ZIP archive.
