Why Check Your SSL Certificate?

SSL certificates are essential for website security and user trust. Regular checks help you:

  • Ensure proper encryption of sensitive data
  • Maintain SEO rankings (Google favors HTTPS sites)
  • Prevent security warnings that deter visitors
  • Stay compliant with security standards
