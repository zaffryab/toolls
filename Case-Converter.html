How to Use the Case Converter

Our case converter tool is simple and intuitive. Follow these steps to transform your text:

1

Enter Your Text

Type or paste your text into the input box at the top of the tool.

2

Select Case Style

Choose the case conversion you want to apply to your text.

3

View Results

Your converted text will appear in the output box below.

4

Copy or Clear

Copy the converted text to clipboard or clear both fields to start over.

Case Conversion Types:

  • UPPERCASE: Converts all text to capital letters
  • lowercase: Converts all text to small letters
  • Title Case: Capitalizes the first letter of each word
  • Sentence case: Capitalizes the first letter of each sentence
  • tOGGLE cASE: Switches the case of each letter
