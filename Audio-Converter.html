Audio Format Guide

MP3

The most popular audio format. Good compression with decent quality. Compatible with all devices.

WAV

Uncompressed, lossless audio format. Best quality but large file sizes. Ideal for professional use.

AAC

Advanced Audio Coding. Better quality than MP3 at similar bitrates. Used by Apple devices.

FLAC

Free Lossless Audio Codec. Compressed without quality loss. Perfect for archiving music.
